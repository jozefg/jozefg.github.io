I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at Aarhus University. I am advised by Lars Birkedal and co-advised by Bas Spitters. I previously studied at Carnegie Mellon University, where I received a B.S. in Computer Science.

I study programming languages, type theory, logic, and their semantics. I am interested in applying semantic methods to develop program logics for sophisticated concurrent programming languages through the Iris project. I also am interested in (modal) dependent type theories, both their syntactic properties and semantics.
