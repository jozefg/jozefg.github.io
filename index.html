Daniel Gratzer

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Computer Science at Aarhus University under Lars Birkedal. I am presently at Oxford University as a visiting researcher and will start as an assistant professor at Aarhus University in the spring of 2025. Have a look at my cv or this photo.

I study programming languages and type theories through the lens of category theory. While I am broadly interested in these subjects, my research is focused on dependent type theory and more specifically on modal or homotopy type theory. Presently, I am interested in applications of modal type theory to synthetic (∞,1)-category theory, using categorical methods to analyze and compare the syntactic behaviors of different modal type theories. I am also interested in applying these insights to the study of programming languages through, for instance, guarded type theory and program logics built on guarded recursion (such as Iris).

If you are a undergraduate or masters student interested in either working with me for an internship or as a Ph.D. student at Aarhus University, I encourage you to send me an email.
